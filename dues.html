Membership Dues & Donations

To remain in good standing with the lodge, members must pay their annual dues in advance on or before April 30 each year【808555561949718†L1050-L1074】. Your dues help sustain our building, educational programs and charitable activities.

Contributions beyond the annual dues are always welcome and appreciated. Donations support our scholarship initiatives and community outreach.

Pay Dues or Donate

You may pay securely by credit card or PayPal using the button below. Enter any amount you wish to contribute.

Note: Paying dues online includes a small processing fee. If you prefer, you may also mail your check to the lodge’s mailing address.

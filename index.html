Fraternal Lodge No. 37

Free and Accepted Masons - McDonough, Georgia

Welcome

Welcome to Fraternal Lodge No. 37, serving the McDonough community since 1844. We are a Masonic lodge committed to the tenets of brotherly love, relief, and truth. Our lodge is proud to uphold the traditions of Freemasonry while embracing new ways to serve our community.

About Freemasonry

Freemasonry is the world's oldest fraternal organization, dedicated to making good men better through moral and spiritual development, charitable work, and community involvement.

Our History

Fraternal Lodge No. 37 received its charter from the Grand Lodge of Georgia in 1844. As one of Georgia's oldest lodges, our history is deeply tied to the foundation and growth of McDonough and Henry County.

What Is Freemasonry?

Freemasonry is the world’s oldest fraternity, built not on politics or profit, but on purpose. For over 300 years, it has brought men together to pursue truth, grow in character, and serve their communities.

Through timeless teachings and powerful symbolism, Freemasonry challenges good men to become great, ethically, spiritually, and personally. We don’t make Masons. We help men build lives of meaning.

Most of us didn’t join for titles or tradition. We joined because something in us was searching, and we found Brothers who helped us become better men.

At its heart, Freemasonry is about:

  • True Friendship: Deep bonds formed through shared experience and trust.
  • Personal Growth: A path to build character and purpose.
  • Community Impact: Quiet service that uplifts neighborhoods and lives.

We aren’t here to argue or divide. We’re here to build, together.

From the East

We don’t claim to have all the answers, but we do know where to start, together. As Master of Fraternal Lodge No. 37, I see men from every walk of life come through our doors searching for something more. And here, they find it, not in secret handshakes, but in shared purpose, challenge, and brotherhood.

Freemasonry isn't about being perfect. It's about being present. If you’re ready to grow, to serve, and to walk with men who hold each other to a higher standard, we will leave the light on for you.

Fraternally,
Jim Robinson, Worshipful Master

What It Takes

If you’ve felt like something was missing, or that the world has dulled your sense of purpose, you might already be searching for what we offer.

We don’t advertise. We don’t recruit. You must knock.

If you’re a man of integrity who believes in something greater than yourself, you’ve already taken the first step. We will walk with you the rest of the way.

The journey begins with dinner. We meet on the 1st and 3rd Thursday of every month.
Dinner is served at 6:30 PM and guests are always welcome.
Come hungry. Come curious. Come ready for more.

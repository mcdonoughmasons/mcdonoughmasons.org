Welcome

Founded in 1844, Fraternal Lodge No. 37 is a community of men dedicated to building themselves and their world through brotherly love, relief and truth. Under the leadership of Worshipful Master Jim Robinson, we seek to preserve nearly two centuries of tradition while embracing modern tools, to promote education, practice charity and leave the world better than we found it【947730938655990†L1070-L1082】.

What is Freemasonry?

Freemasonry is more than an ancient fraternity — it’s a journey of self‑discovery. Men of honor and integrity meet in our lodge to become better husbands, fathers and citizens while giving back to the communities they love. The craft encourages self‑reflection and service, helping us to practice the values of equality, hope and charity【947730938655990†L1098-L1107】.

Our History

Our lodge is part of an institution whose roots reach back to medieval stonemasons and cathedral builders. Freemasonry is the oldest fraternal organization in the world, and Georgia freemasons trace their beginnings to between 1730 and 1734. The Grand Lodge of Georgia was formally organized on December 16, 1786【947730938655990†L1124-L1133】. Today, nearly 180 years after our charter, Fraternal Lodge No. 37 continues to uphold those traditions of excellence and service.
